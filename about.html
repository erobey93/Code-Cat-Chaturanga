About Me

Hi! I'm Emily! I am an aspiring software engineer with a deep passion for psychology. I have a BS in psychology and social science with a focus on Attachment theory, specifically in non-traditional environments such as the workplace and school. I had intended to pursue a PhD in clinical psychology, but due to a plethora of reasons my path changed. After graduating with my first degree I took a year to work and collect myself. I used this time to explore other career options which included attending a very short coding boot camp. Empowered and excited by the boot camp and inspired by a few good friends I decided to pursue a path that has always intrigued me: software engineering.

Given my perpetually curious nature I couldn't help but want to further explore all of the concepts that I am learning as I complete my BS in Software Engineering while further understanding the crossover between psychology and software engineering. In addition to discussing topics pertaining to software engineering, psychology and other cross-over disciplines I wanted to build a website from scratch while possibly connecting with other people on similar journeys. I've been blogging and building websites (from templates mostly) since I was young and writing has always been a natural extensions of understanding, processing and sharing my world, so building a website and blog was the perfect outlet for me! All in all, I don't have any big end goals and I'm not doing this for anyone else. I just wanted my own little corner on the web to build, learn and document it all.
